UNITED ASCEP CHURCH SCHOOLS

S.E.C. REG. NO. CN201229718 | TIN NO. 423-230-302-000

We, the Christian educators across the land, having had organized ourselves to becoming true harbinger and forerunner of Basic Street-Smart Christian Education not necessarily catering only to our church members. But in willful eagerness and enthusiasm to be of assistance to children from all walks of life across the board across the nation. Recognizing the need and importance of basic education for all, which is occupying a central place in the mandate of the currentadministration, we are putting our hands together that we may in our little capacity help out. As community leaders and catalysts of holistic development, we are looking forward to eventual economic progression by way of systematic human development as framework for future leaders and torch bearers of democratic and righteous principles. This could very well be our little contribution to government’s over-all agenda that our brothers and sisters in our little corner of the world may benefit from.

That we may create and establish the right kind of venue rendering basic street-smart christian education in all possible areas across the nation deemed appropriate for pastors and christian leaders may bestow and impart effeciently and effectively; That through sheer nobility of purposes and actual delivery of needs on a timely fashion, involvement and enthused participation from local lgu officials likewise those from corporate communities may become apparent in as many towns, cities and provinces in accepting this initiative that many children from given communities may directly benefit;That by doing so, activities bringing about appropriate income for pastors and church leaders are made available, making such noble undertaking become proper tool and right venue for economic sufficiency and adequacy for our otherwise income deficient pastors, church leaders, volunteers, elders, …and appropriately give way to zealous and fervent career for pastor’s wives and able church members;That church schools may multiply and flourish in many barangays across the nation.

Dr. felix p. salegon, Jr. DD., DCEM United ASCEP President / C.E.O
